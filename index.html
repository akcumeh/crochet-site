Projects

I make more toys / amigurumi than other kinds of items. Here, you can see some of the projects I've completed.

I started off making wearables such as socks and sweaters, but then I discovered I could worry less about materials and sizing when making toys/amigurumi.

Besides, they're fun to make and very cute when completed! Don't you agree?
